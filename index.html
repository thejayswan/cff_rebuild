About CrossFit Flagstaff

Building Resilient Humans

We are a community first, functional fitness facility dedicated to providing the best hour of your day. We have a proven track record of delivering results. Our goal is to make you fit for life and provide the best possible environment to make fitness fun, approachable, and effective. What we do in the gym exists to support whatever you do outside the gym.

We do the work so you don't have to. Our classes are coach led with warm ups, cool downs, movement prep, etc. You never have to worry about what you're doing that day. Just show up, work hard, meet some friends, and have a great time getting better together.

Enjoy our world class facility, equipment, coaching staff, program, and all things CrossFit.
